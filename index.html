About

Tim Henson is a genre-defying guitarist and co-founder of the progressive rock band Polyphia. Known for his innovative playing style, genre-blending compositions, and modern aesthetic, Tim continues to push the boundaries of what guitar music can be.

Guitarist, Producer & Visionary Artist
